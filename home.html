Who we are? What we do? Growth Be Part News Presence

We are dedicated to protecting the nature. We believe that nature is the very core of our responsibility as a human. In response to many global challenges and with the help of the well minded people we form this institution so that together we can create a timely solution to answer the challenge of global warming. Save the Earth, Make a Change is our main dictum. Each individuals has the capacity to help our enviroment stay healthy. We believe that we can make a change through strong synergy for the common cause.

We live with nature and so we have to be responsible steward. We do dialectical discourse that aim to have a diversified result to answer many types of environmental crisis. We do research in a specific area to analyse the cause of the problem so that we can deliver an exceptional solution. We commit ourselves to the nature. We protect our common good. We believe we can make it for our nature.

Since the time we founded the organisation we have now delivered and seen the result of our labor. We have five hundred partners, one thousand advocates and 300 headquarters globally. We are just starting the race, we will continue it for our goal to achieve an environment free from pollution.

Everyone is welcome to be part of our goal to save the earth and make a change. If you have the passion to be a good steward of our environment, then you are very much welcome to be part of our advocate team. Let us make a change. Protect our nature and be a responsible steward.

The global challenges recognized Ir Verde as one of the effective institution for protection and conservation of world's natural resources. Its impact is widely known in the African continent and Asia Pacific. Many local and national agencies with the same advocacy acknowledged the change Ir Verde has made all throughout the years.

At present Ir Verde is making known its presence in Africa, Asia, Europe, Australia and the Americas. It has headquarters in Canada, England, United States of America, France, Germany, Scotland, Philippines, Malaysia, Singapore, Mexico, Costa Rica, Puerto Rico, South Africa, Kenya, Ghana, Cameroon, Senegal, Mauritius, Australia, Papua New Guinea, Hawai, Tokelau and Tuvalu. In the coming years new headquarters in Japan and China is to be opened. We are always open to work in the countries where there is abuse in natural resources. We have realised that the environmental problem can result to humanitarian crisis. That is why we will continue to make our presence known internationally to establish environmental awareness.
